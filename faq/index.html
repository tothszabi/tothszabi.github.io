Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Here is everything you need to know about Pilates Live.

Getting Started

Yes! We believe that health and movement should be accessible to everyone. All our pre-recorded classes are completely free to watch and follow along. We are supported by our community and occasional sponsorships, allowing us to keep the core content free for you.
For most of our 'Mat Pilates' classes, all you need is a comfortable mat. Some specialized classes might use props like a resistance band, a Pilates ball, or light weights, but we always list the required equipment in the class description. We also offer plenty of 'no equipment' classes!
Welcome! We recommend starting with our 'Beginner Series' or filtering for 'Easier' level classes. These sessions focus on the fundamental principles of Pilates, such as breathing, alignment, and core engagement, moving at a pace that allows you to learn the technique safely.

Classes & Training

Consistency is key! Joseph Pilates recommended doing Pilates at least 3-4 times a week. However, even 10-20 minutes a day can make a huge difference. Listen to your body and find a routine that fits your lifestyle. We have classes ranging from 10 minutes to an hour to help you fit movement into your day.
Pilates is often used for rehabilitation, but it's crucial to consult with your doctor or physiotherapist before starting any new exercise program. Our instructor, Vicky, is a qualified Physiotherapist, and many classes are designed with safety in mind. However, please read the class descriptions for any specific contraindications and stop if you feel pain.
We have a dedicated 'Prenatal Pilates' section! These classes are specifically designed to be safe for expectant mothers. However, general Pilates classes may include movements that are not suitable for pregnancy (like lying on your stomach or deep abdominal work). Always check with your healthcare provider first.

Account & Technical

No, you can watch all our videos without an account. However, creating a free account allows you to save your favorite classes, track your progress, and receive our weekly newsletter with tips and new class alerts.
Our website is mobile-friendly, so you can cast to your TV using AirPlay (for Apple devices) or Chromecast (for Android/Chrome). You can also access the site directly through the web browser on most smart TVs.
Please check your internet connection first. If the connection is stable, try lowering the video quality using the settings gear icon on the video player. Clearing your browser cache or trying a different browser can also help resolve playback issues.
