Our Classes

Find the perfect class for your journey, from beginner basics to advanced power flows.

Group Sessions

Join our supportive community in one of our group classes. Each session is designed to help you progress safely and effectively.

Beginner Pilates

Perfect for those just starting their Pilates journey. We focus on the fundamental principles, building a strong foundation for your practice.

  • Core activation basics
  • Posture alignment
  • Breathing techniques
  • Gentle flexibility

Intermediate Flow

Ready to step up the challenge? This class introduces more fluid movements and increases the intensity to build strength and endurance.

  • Fluid transitions
  • Increased resistance
  • Balance challenges
  • Full body integration

Advanced Power

Designed for experienced practitioners who want to push their limits. Expect complex sequences and a high-energy workout.

  • High intensity
  • Complex choreography
  • Advanced repertoire
  • Muscular endurance

Post-Natal Recovery

A supportive environment for new mums to reconnect with their bodies. Safe, gentle exercises to aid recovery after pregnancy.

  • Pelvic floor strengthening
  • Diastasis recti safe
  • Upper back release
  • Relaxation focus
