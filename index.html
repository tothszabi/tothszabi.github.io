About Pilates Live

Pilates Live is all about making Pilates accessible. It is run by Vicky, an experienced Physiotherapist and qualified Pilates instructor.

In her job as an NHS physiotherapist Vicky would often recommend Pilates exercises to support patients' rehabilitation. This sparked a love of Pilates and a great understanding to its wider benefits.

We hope you enjoy these free online Pilates classes.

Why Pilates? Why Pilates Live?

Pilates is a powerful low-impact workout that builds full-body strength, core stability, improved posture, and flexibility.

The benefits are broad and include:

  • A stronger core
  • Improved flexibility and mobility
  • Improved posture and alignment
  • Better mental wellbeing

And doing Pilates from home—especially when it's free—takes those benefits even further.
